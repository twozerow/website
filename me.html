MELINA

The one who walks alongside flame, Shall one day meet the road of Destined Death.
Melina is a spirit, and by her own account, her physical body was burned a long time ago, so you can see that her skin is covered with intense burn marks.
In the game, all Melina actions seem to be to achieve her mission, so the dialogue with her are mechanical dialogue, but in the end she ignited herself to burn the Erdtree, but tenderly first let the player into a deep sleep, so that the player can feel that she actually has real feelings.
