Limgrave
Melina

Melina was born at the foot of the Erdtree and describes her mother as within the Erdtree.Although it is never stated, it can be inferred that her mother is Queen Marika the Eternal. During Boc's questline she asks if "being born of a mother" means acting in certain ways, which makes the specifics of her origins unclear.

Stranded Souls

Stranded Souls can be found throughout the Lands Between, offering short pieces of dialogue which serve to add context to the lore and story.

White Mask Varré

Varré is an enigmatic man who dresses in the garb of a war surgeon.The Tarnished first encounters him in Limgrave, after exiting the Stranded Graveyard, where he offers them guidance, noticing that they are not accompanied by a Finger Maiden.

Bloody Finger Hunter Yura

Yura is a warrior who dresses in the garb reminiscent of that worn in the Land of Reeds.He is a self-proclaimed hunter of Bloody Fingersand a prior associate of Eleonora, Violet Bloody Finger, whom he likely had strong feelings for.

Sorceress Sellen

A former scholar of the Academy of Raya Lucaria. Sellen was considered to be the Academy's most promising sorceress, however she was expelled following accusations of unspeakable treatment of her fellow scholars, under the name of the Graven Witch.

Kenneth Haight

Kenneth Haight is a nobleman and claims to be next in line as the rightful ruler of Limgrave. He resided in a fort known as Fort Haight, and is known for his considerable largesse, at least by his own account. Godrick the Grafted assumed control of Limgrave following the Shattering, and at some point sent a knight commander to seize Fort Haight.

Blaidd the Half- Wolf

Blaidd was created by Ranni's own Two Fingers as a vassal to Lunar Princess Ranni and to be incapable of treachery towards her, as she was one of the three Empyreans candidates chosen by the Greater Will, along with Miquella and Malenia, to eventually replace their mother Queen Marika the Eternal as the God of a new age.

Boc the Seamster

Boc is a demi-human seamster from Limgrave. Boc's mother was a seamstress, and the two very close until she passed away. Boc always wanted to be like his mother and inherited her sowing kit. Boc lived with a demi-human tribe in a cave along Limgrave's western coast.

Ranni the Witch

Originally known as Lunar Princess Ranni, she was the daughter of Radagon, a champion of the Golden Order, and Queen Rennala, head of both the Carian Royal Family and the Academy of Raya Lucaria. She had two older brothers, Radahn and Rykard.

Patches

A Tarnished who leads a group of bandits, operating out of Murkwater Cave in Limgrave. Patches is a nomadic and free-spirited individual with a proclivity for tricking people and robbing them of their possessions.

Stormhill
Roderika, Spirit Tuner

Roderika alone survived, taking refuge in Stormhill Shack. It is here that she meets the Tarnished. She beseeches the Tarnished to bring a message to her grafted companions, whom she refers to as chrysalids, assuring them that she loves them and will join them soon.

Knight Bernahl

Residing in the Warmaster's Shack of Limgrave, Knight Bernahl is a tarnished merchant who sells a number of Ashes of War.

Alexander, Warrior Jar

Alexander is a living jar who travels the Lands Between, honing his strength in order to become a great warrior. Alexander was created to be a warrior vessel, and the remains of many great warriors reside within him.

D, Hunter of the Dead

Darian, more commonly known as D, Hunter of the Dead is an undead hunter and an ally of Gurranq, the Beast Clergyman. Darian has a twin brother by the name of Devin, who was also called D. While Darien and Devin had different minds and bodies, they shared a single soul, and were reviled as cursed beings. The twins found sanctuary in the Golden Order, the only institution to accept them.

Finger Reader Crone

Whenever the Tarnished comes across one them, they become overly excited and beg to see their fingers, in order to read their future. They have the gift of foresight, telling vague and cryptic clues, usually about the area they reside in or a quest yet to be undertaken.

Weeping Peninsula
Irina of Morne

Irina is the daughter of Edgar, castellan of Castle Morne. Her eyesight has been weak since birth and she wears a blindfold around her eyes.

Castellan Edgar

Edgar is the warden of Castle Morne, a duty entrusted to him by Godrick the Grafted, who currently presides over Limgrave

Stormveil Castle
Gatekeeper Gostoc

Gostoc is a servant working in Stormveil Castle, in the role of gatekeeper. Gostoc detests his master, Godrick the Grafted, who always looked down on and mistreated him

Sorcerer Rogier

Rogier is a sorcerer, who is first encountered as an optional summon against Margit, the Fell Omen, he is later found within a church searching for knowledge in Stormveil Castle.

Nepheli Loux

Nepheli Loux is a Tarnished warrior from the Badlands. In her infancy, Nepheli lived through murder and pillage unchecked At some point, she lost the guidance of grace, and was taken in by Gideon Ofnir, who became her foster father.

Roundtable Hold
Sir Gideon Ofnir

Gideon Ofnir is the Tarnished leader of Roundtable Hold and the adoptive father of Nepheli Loux.Called to the Lands Between by the guidance of Grace, Gideon wishes to become Elden Lord.

Brother Corhyn

Brother Corhyn is a cleric of the Golden Order. At some point, Corhyn made his way to Roundtable Hold, where he teaches the incantations of the Two Fingers to Tarnished of the Roundtable, hoping that one of them might one day claim the title of Elden Lord, so that Corhyn might council them.

Knight Diallos

Diallos Hoslow is a scion of House Hoslow and the younger brother of Juno Hoslow. From a young age, Diallos was all talk and no trousers, with an inability to commit to action. As a result, Juno inherited the Hoslow legacy without resistance.

Ensha of the Royal Remains

Ensha is the unspeaking adherent of Sir Gideon Ofnir, typically found leaning on the wall outside his master's study in Roundtable Hold. Ensha wears armour graced with golden human bones, which are said to have belonged to a soulless king, the lord of the lost and desperate, known as Ensha.

Smithing Master Hewg

Hewg is a blacksmith with the appearance of a Misbegotten. He is a prisoner of Roundtable Hold, tasked with smithing weapons for the Tarnished.

Fia

Fia was once a Deathbed Companion. She would receive the warmth and vigor from champions, and then lay with the remains of an exalted noble, to grant him another chance at life.

Finger Reader Enia

Enia is a Finger Reader, much like the Finger Reader Crones scattered across the Lands Between. She interprets the words of the Two Fingers, envoys of the Greater Will.

Liurnia of the Lakes
Lightseeker Hyetta

Hyetta is a blind woman travelling alone through the Lands Between, following a distant light which she believes will lead her to her duty as a Finger Maiden.

Sorcerer Thops

A former student of the Academy of Raya Lucaria, Thops was considered a "bluntstone", lacking any talent for sorcery, yet he looked up to Sorceress Sellen prior to her expulsion from the Academy.

Rya the Scout

A young woman who works as a scout for the Recusants of Volcano Manor, tasked with recruiting Tarnished to the cause. Rya is the adopted daughter of Tanith, although she believes her to be her true mother.

Blackguard Big Boggart

Boggart is an ex-convict who was called to the Lands Between by the Guidance of Grace.

Jar- Bairn

Jar-Bairn is a young Living Jar who lives in the secluded village of Jarburg in Liurnia of the Lakes. Like his uncle Alexander, he is a warrior jar, and strives to become a champion just like Alexander.

Miriel, Pastor of Vows

Miriel, Pastor of Vows (insert Japanese) is the steward of the Church of Vows in eastern Liurnia of the Lakes. A gigantic, ancient tortoise wearing a large white mitre, Miriel is a gentle soul, and will happily assist the The Tarnished (Protagonist) on their journey.

Old Albus

An elderly resident of the Village of the Albinaurics, Albus was one of the few survivors of the massacre of the village at the hands of Gideon's men, and the only one to retain his sanity.

Latenna the Albinauric

Latenna is an Albinauric woman and a deft archer. Latenna and her wolf companion Lobo began their journey in the lands of the Haligtree, a sanctuary for Albinaurics.

War Counselor Iji

good-natured troll who once served the Carian Royal Family as a blacksmith.

Pidia

Pidia, Carian Servant is an elderly Albinauric who serves as keeper of the puppets in Caria Manor. A meek and ingratiating fellow, he will attempt to gain favor with The Tarnished (Protagonist) by selling them rare items from his storeroom.

Ranni the Witch

Originally known as Lunar Princess Ranni, she was the daughter of Radagon, a champion of the Golden Order, and Queen Rennala, head of both the Carian Royal Family and the Academy of Raya Lucaria. She had two older brothers, Radahn and Rykard.

Preceptor Seluvis

A preceptor in the service of Ranni the Witch, the last princess of the Carian Royal Family.

Academy of Raya Lucaria
Rennala, Queen of the Full Moon

As a young astrologer who gazed at the night sky and always chased the stars,Rennala would encounter an enchanting full moon that would one day bewitch the Academy of Raya Lucaria.

Altus Plateau
Dung Eater

The Dung Eater once saw guidance and a ring that would appear at the end of his journey. His vision is of a curse he calls the "pox upon life itself" or the "reviled blessing",with everyone sustaining it in despair and their souls not being able to return to the Erdtree.

Goldmask

Goldmask is a Tarnished scholar and staunch Golden Order fundamentalist.

Primeval Sorcerer Azur

Azur is one of the founding glintstone sorcerersand the originator of the Karolos Conspectus, considered to be the oldest and most venerable lineage of study at the Academy of Raya Lucaria, dedicated to pursuing the mysteries of comets.

Volcano Manor
Tanith

Tanith is the consort of Praetor Rykard and the leader of the Recusants, a band of Tarnished who have taken up arms against the Erdtree and vowed to hunt down their own kind.

Caelid
Sage Gowry

Gowry is an elderly sage dwelling in the blighted Caelid Wilds. When the Empyrean Malenia unleashed the Scarlet Rot during her battle with General Radahn, she permanently blighted Caelid.

Millicent

A red haired woman afflicted by Scarlet Rot. Millicent and her sisters were found in the Swamp of Aeonia in Caelid by Sage Gowry, who raised them as his daughters.

Primeval Sorcerer Lusat

Lusat is one of the founding glintstone sorcerers and the originator of the Olivinus Conspectus.He hailed from Sellia, a town of magic-practicing assassins in Caelid.

Castellan Jerren

Jerren is the elderly castellan of Redmane Castle. In the past, Jerren was a nomadic warrior who became a guest of the Carian Royal Family.

Greyoll's Dragonbarrow
Gurranq, Beast Clergyman

Gurranq is a hulking, bestial clergyman who resides in the Bestial Sanctum in north-eastern Caelid. Long ago, his terrifying ferocity earned him the name "Death of the Demigods.

Nokron, Eternal City
D, Beholder of Death

Devin, more commonly known as "D", is the twin-brother of Darian, who is also known as D. The two twins had separate minds and bodies, but shared a single soul.

Mountaintops of the Giants
Shabriri

Shabriri, the most reviled man in all history, is said to have begun the sickness of the flame of frenzy.[ For the crime of slander, he had his eyes gouged out, and the blight of the flame of frenzy later came to dwell within the empty sockets.

Consecrated Snowfield
Phillia

Phillia is a gigantic first-generation albinauric. She can be found at the end of Latenna's quest at the Consecrated Snowfield.
