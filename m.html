Malenia

...Heed my words. I am Malenia. Blade of Miquella. And I have never known defeat.
As a well-known valkyrie in the Elden ring, Malenia possesses a high level of swordsmanship, and although her innate corrupting power has caused her to lose one hand and one foot, it has not diminished her fighting ability.
Malenia loves her brother Miquella and protects him, so she is called Blade of Miquella, but because of this, she lost her mind when Miquella disappeared and fought with the Starscourge Radahn, resulting in both defeats, and had to return to the Elphael to recuperate, and finally turned into an flower waiting to be reborn after fighting with the player-managed tarnished.
