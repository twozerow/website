Some tips Make you play Eazy

Some techniques, weapons that make you to simply pass the game.

Elden Ring as a soulslike game, the degree of difficulty is very high, so some players continue to explore how to make the game easier, and the following are some of the techniques found in the game.

Rune Grinding

Rune is a very important thing in the game because it allows you to upgrade your abilities and make you stronger, and the following is a way to grind rune.

First, go to the place in the film：Lenne,s Rise, then ride a horse to the direction of the film, to the location will appear an iron ball rolling towards you, then just go around the iron ball will fall off the cliff, you can get 2000 rune.

As the game transmission will refresh the map mechanism, so we can use the transmission to infinite grind rune.

Meteorite Staff & Rock Sling

Meteorite Staff & Rock Sling are great tools for players playing the Mage route, as they not only do very high damage, but also consume little magic power, so they are a good choice if you want to get through the early bosses easily.

The way to get the Meteorite Staff and Rock Sling is to go to the place marked on the map after coming out of the Gallia Crystal Tunnel, and as long as you pay attention not to kill the monsters in the tunnel, there is no difficulty in getting out.

Ash of War：Bloodhound,s step

Ash of War：Bloodhound,s step is a very useful skill, its effect is to make you disappear from the original place, and then appear in the direction you are moving forward, so it is very suitable for fast approaching enemies or dodge skills, its use is to ask the blacksmith to attach it to the weapon, so that it can be used.

Bloodhound,s step is obtained by killing the Night,s cavalry on the bridge next to the Wizard's Tower in Lenne,s Rise. Here you should note that the Night,s cavalry will only appear at night, so you have to let time pass at the site of Grace before he can appear.

Death,s Poker

Death,s Poker is a very high damage weapon, not only high physical damage, can hit the hard straight, and also with the effect of freezing, can make the enemy's movement becomes slow, so that players can more easily respond to the enemy's attack.

In addition, this weapon's ability value requirement is very low, allowing players to use it when they get it early.

The way to get Death,s Poker is to go to the site of Grace called Sothern Aeonia Swamp Bank, and defeat the death rite bird located nearby to get it, and here you should also note that the death rite bird like the Night,s cavalry will only appear at night, so the same must first turn in the site of Grace fast to night.

Radagon,s Soreseal

Radagon,s Soreseal is a very good talisman, although it will make you suffer higher damage, but can make your Vigor、Endurance、Strength and Dexterity both plus five, can be said to install the equivalent of level 20, is one of the top talisman in this game.

It is obtained from the site of Grace called Fort Faroth, Enter the castle and climb up, then jump into the second floor and jump onto the wooden board with the giant rat on it, and finally jump down again to get it.
